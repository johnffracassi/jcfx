Strategy Miner
==============
1. search a chart for all the good buy/sell points
   - find and mark all the local minimums/maximums

2. determine how the indicators performed in the lead up to the optimum price points
   - check the effect of news items/outcomes
   - monitor performance of RSI/CCI/etc... (direction, acceleration, difference)
   - time of day, day of week


Data Store Manager
====================
GUI Application to manage the Data Store
- Download new data
- Show which date ranges, datasources, currency pair are populated
- Validate data
